Odoo Discounts On Invoices And Bills

Provide discounts on invoices in Odoo!!

By default, you can only add a percentage discount on invoice and bill lines. Odoo Discounts On Invoices And Bills adds a fixed discount type for invoice & bill lines. The global discount category that can be fixed or percent type and apply on the entire invoice or bill.

    With our Module

  • The module facilitates the Odoo Users to set discounts in Invoices and bills in Odoo.
